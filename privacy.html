Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

What we collect

Nothing. Spiral Buddy does not store your voice, transcripts, or conversations.

How it works

  • Your voice is processed in real-time to generate responses
  • Audio is deleted immediately after processing
  • No recordings are saved on your device or our servers

What we store locally

  • Your display name (optional)
  • Your character preference

These are stored only on your device using secure, encrypted storage. We never see or access this data.

Third-party services

We use the following services to process your voice in real-time:

  • OpenAI Whisper (speech-to-text) — processes audio, does not store it
  • Anthropic Claude (responses) — processes text, does not store it
  • ElevenLabs (text-to-speech) — generates voice, does not store it

Audio and text are transmitted securely and discarded immediately after processing.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any personal data. Ever.

Children's privacy

Spiral Buddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Your rights

Since we don't store any of your data, there's nothing to delete or export. Your conversations exist only in the moment.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at spiralbuddyapp@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
